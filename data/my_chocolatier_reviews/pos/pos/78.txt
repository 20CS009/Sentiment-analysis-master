Die and go to Chocolate! Chocolate Covered is one of SF's unique hidden gems. A chocolate store with 800 different kinds of chocolate from around the world. Jack carries chocolate bars you won't find in other stores and he and the helpful staff will give you tastes if you can't decide. They also have handmade gift boxes with a unique antiquing process using photos from around SF and popular culture. It's the perfect place to buy a gift for yourself and someone else! If you love chocolate but don't believe in heaven, you will now!
